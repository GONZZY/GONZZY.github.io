Steak Recipe


Description

Itâ€™s easy to make THE BEST sirloin steak marinade with just a few simple ingredients. Quickly whisk the marinade ingredients together, marinate the steak, then toss it on the grill for the best flavor!

Ingredients

  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Low-sodium soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Honey
  • Dried Italian seasoning
  • Dried mustard

Steps

  1. Combine balsamic vinegar, low-sodium soy sauce, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, honey, Italian seasoning and mustard in a gallon size zipper bag or large bowl.
  2. Add the steaks and marinade for 20 minutes, or up to 8 hours.
